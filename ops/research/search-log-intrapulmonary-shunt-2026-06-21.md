## Inclusion Criteria
- Direct measurement or discussion of intrapulmonary shunt (Qs/Qt, shunt fraction, right-to-left shunt, IPAVA)
- Study population: Long COVID, post-COVID, ME/CFS, or exercise-induced hypoxemia
- Publication type: peer-reviewed articles (preprints only if recent + highly relevant)
- English language
- Published 2020–2026 (for COVID-related); earlier included for physiological mechanism papers

## Exclusion Criteria
- Acute ARDS/mechanical ventilation studies only (no relevance to post-acute or chronic phase)
- Animal studies
- Letters/commentaries without original data (except key mechanistic reviews)
- Pure imaging methodology papers without clinical correlation
- Cardiac shunt studies without pulmonary component
