## Papers Found: 5 total (1 already in bib, 4 new)

### 1. Sommerfelt et al. 2024 — FUNCAP (INDEX PAPER, already in bib)
- **Journal**: Journal of Clinical Medicine (MDPI), 13(12):3486
- **DOI**: 10.3390/jcm13123486 | **PMID**: 38930014 | **PMCID**: PMC11204454
- **Design**: Questionnaire development + validation (5 survey rounds + test-retest)
- **Samples**: Norwegian n=1,263 ME/CFS + 178 HC; International English n=1,387 ME/CFS
- **Certainty**: 0.70
- **Summary**: The foundational FUNCAP paper. Developed two questionnaire versions (FUNCAP55, FUNCAP27) across 8 domains of functional capacity, specifically designed for PEM-affected patients. Novel "consequence-based" questioning ("what happens when you do X" not "can you do X"). Good reliability/validity, negligible floor/ceiling effects. Available in 8 languages under CC BY license.

### 2. Weigel et al. 2024 — Symptom Clusters ME/CFS & PCC (NEW, added to bib)
- **Journal**: Quality of Life Research, 33(12):3229-3243
- **DOI**: 10.1007/s11136-024-03794-x | **PMID**: 39361124
- **Design**: Pilot cross-sectional
- **Sample**: n=90 (ME/CFS n=60, PCC n=30)
- **Certainty**: 0.50
- **Summary**: Investigates symptom clusters and patient-reported outcomes in ME/CFS vs PCC. Autonomic symptoms (dyspnoea) most negatively associated with QoL/functioning. No unique clusters for either condition. Demonstrates need for standardized functional capacity tools across PEM-related conditions.

### 3. Jason & Chee 2025 — Brief PEM Measure (NEW, added to bib)
- **Journal**: Exploration of Neuroprotective Therapy
- **DOI**: 10.37349/ent.2025.1004116
- **Design**: Questionnaire development
- **Certainty**: 0.50
- **Summary**: Develops a brief PEM-specific measure. References FUNCAP as the established standard for functional capacity assessment in ME/CFS. Complementary rather than competing instrument.

### 4. Goxhaj et al. 2026 — Long COVID Outcome Measures (NEW, added to bib)
- **Journal**: Lancet Infectious Diseases, 26(1):13-15
- **DOI**: 10.1016/S1473-3099(25)00665-6 | **PMID**: 41213281
- **Design**: Commentary/Editorial
- **Certainty**: 0.40
- **Summary**: Patient-led research collaborative commentary arguing poor outcome measure selection drives negative Long COVID trial results. Highlights FUNCAP as a functional capacity scale designed for PEM patients. Notable endorsement in a top-tier journal.

### 5. Meadows et al. 2025 — LIFT Trial Protocol (NEW, added to bib)
- **Journal**: Research Square (preprint)
- **DOI**: 10.21203/rs.3.rs-5626167
- **Design**: RCT protocol (pyridostigmine + LDN for ME/CFS)
- **Certainty**: 0.30
- **Summary**: First clinical trial to adopt FUNCAP-55 as primary outcome measure. Validates FUNCAP's suitability as an interventional trial endpoint. Preprint only; no results yet.

## Key Gaps in Literature
1. **No independent external validation** of FUNCAP by a different research group (as of 2026-04-23)
2. **No longitudinal sensitivity-to-change data** published
3. **No direct comparison** of FUNCAP against other functional capacity tools (SF-36, WHODAS, etc.) in ME/CFS populations
4. **LIFT trial results pending** — will provide first interventional use data
5. Literature is **sparse** — FUNCAP was published in June 2024, so adoption is still early
