### Inclusion Criteria
- Peer-reviewed original research, systematic reviews, or meta-analyses
- English language
- CCM as primary measurement method
- Relevance to cross-disease SFN biomarker argument
- Not already cited in the paper (Azcue 2023, Azcue 2025, CaĂ±adas 2023 excluded)

### Exclusion Criteria
- Case reports, conference abstracts, preprints
- Non-human studies
- Duplicate or overlapping cohorts
- Papers focusing solely on ophthalmic outcomes (not neuropathy)
- Papers already cited in the main paper
