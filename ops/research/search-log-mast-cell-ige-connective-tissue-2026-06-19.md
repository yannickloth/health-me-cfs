## Inclusion Criteria
- English language
- Addresses at least one step in the mechanistic chain: mast cell mediator → ECM/connective tissue effect
- Or: clinical association between MCAS/MCD and hEDS/HSD/hypermobility
- Or: anti-IgE therapy (omalizumab) in MCAS
- Or: competing mechanisms for hypermobility (genetic, TNXB)
- Or: safety/harm related to MC-targeted therapies
- Peer-reviewed journal or recent preprint (2024-2026)

## Exclusion Criteria
- Non-English
- Irrelevant tissue context (e.g. tumor mast cells only)
- MCAS diagnostic criteria discussions with no mechanistic or treatment data
- Pediatric-only primary atopic disorders (PAD)
- Animal studies exclusively on non-connective-tissue systems without human relevance
- Duplicate entries already present in mast-cell.bib or connective-tissue.bib
