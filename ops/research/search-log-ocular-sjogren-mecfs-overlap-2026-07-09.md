## Inclusion Criteria
- Direct evidence of ocular surface pathology (tear proteomics, CCM, dry eye) in Sjögren's syndrome, fibromyalgia, or fatigue-spectrum conditions
- Corneal confocal microscopy studies linking SFN to autoimmune or fatigue-spectrum conditions
- Tear-fluid proteomics/cytokine studies with relevance to systemic inflammation
- English language
- Published 2000–2026 (with emphasis on 2020–2026)
- Both peer-reviewed and preprints (if highly relevant)

## Exclusion Criteria
- Papers focused only on the general SjS-ME/CFS overlap (M3 muscarinic antibodies, seronegative Sjögren, sicca-fatigue without ocular-specific data) — already integrated
- Animal studies only (without human relevance pathway)
- Pure treatment trials without mechanistic data
- Opinion pieces without original data
- Non-English without English abstract
- Acute COVID-19 ocular findings only (no chronic/fatigue context)
