## Inclusion Criteria
- ADHD-ASD comorbidity prevalence or mechanism studies
- Shared genetic/biological pathway evidence
- Link to fatigue, chronic illness, or ME/CFS where available
- Peer-reviewed, meta-analyses, large registries, GWAS, twin studies preferred

## Exclusion Criteria
- Single-case studies
- Purely behavioral intervention studies without biological mechanism
- Studies without comorbidity focus (ADHD-only or ASD-only)
