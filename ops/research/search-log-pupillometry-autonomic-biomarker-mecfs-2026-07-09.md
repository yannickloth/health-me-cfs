## Inclusion Criteria

- Direct pupillometry/PLR measurement in any autonomic disorder
- Cross-disease pupillometry studies in conditions comorbid with or overlapping ME/CFS (MS, diabetic neuropathy, CRPS, PAF, PCS)
- Autoantibody studies against M3 muscarinic or alpha-1 adrenergic receptors (pupil autonomic receptors) in ME/CFS or related conditions
- Method papers validating pupillometry as autonomic biomarker
- Null/negative results (mandatory)
- English language preferred
- Published 1997–2026

## Exclusion Criteria

- Pupillometry used only for sedation/nociception monitoring (surgical anesthesia)
- Purely ophthalmological studies without autonomic focus (cataract, glaucoma surgical outcomes)
- Animal studies only
- Preprints without peer review (unless highly relevant and recent)
- Opinion pieces without original data or systematic review methodology
- Listening effort/cognitive load studies in hearing-impaired populations (no autonomic disease component)
