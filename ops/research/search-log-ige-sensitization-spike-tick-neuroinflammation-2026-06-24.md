## Inclusion Criteria
- Addresses at least one step in the chain: antigen-specific IgE → mast cell activation → BBB/neuroinflammation → cognition
- Or: viral/bacterial infection triggering allergic sensitization or IgE class switching
- Or: alpha-gal syndrome / tick-induced specific IgE
- Or: IgE / Th2 / allergy in ME/CFS specifically
- Or: null/negative studies on IgE in ME/CFS
- Or: competing non-IgE mast cell activation mechanisms
- Peer-reviewed (or recent 2025-2026 preprint with strong methodology)
- English language

## Exclusion Criteria
- Non-English
- Unrelated to hypothesis chain (e.g. general respiratory virus epidemiology without IgE)
- Tumor immunology without relevance to mast cell → neuroinflammation
- Vaccine safety studies without IgE mechanism data
- Pediatric-only atopic disease natural history
- Duplicate entries from prior mast-cell literature searches (Wilson 2026, Ganesh 2024, Martin 2019 from 2026-06-19 search)
