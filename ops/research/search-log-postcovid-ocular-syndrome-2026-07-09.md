## Inclusion Criteria
- Direct evidence of ocular/small-fiber/autonomic pathology in post-COVID or ME/CFS
- Corneal confocal microscopy studies in post-COVID or ME/CFS
- Tear fluid proteomics in post-COVID (methodology or clinical)
- Systematic reviews or large cohort studies on post-COVID ocular symptoms
- Pupillary function studies in post-COVID
- English language only
- Published 2020–2026

## Exclusion Criteria
- Pre-COVID ocular disease unrelated to post-infectious mechanisms
- Acute COVID-19 ocular findings only (no long-term follow-up)
- Animal studies only
- Preprints without peer review (unless highly relevant and recent)
- Opinion pieces without original data
- Studies of anosmia/ageusia only (olfactory, not ocular)
