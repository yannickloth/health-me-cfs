## Inclusion Criteria
- taVNS/tVNS as intervention for PCC, long COVID, post-COVID fatigue
- Any study design (RCT, open-label, single-arm, feasibility, systematic review)
- Peer-reviewed preferred; preprints included only if highly relevant and recent
- English language
- Published 2021–2026

## Exclusion Criteria
- Implanted VNS studies (cervical/thoracic) — different risk/benefit profile
- Animal studies
- Conference abstracts without full text
- Purely mechanistic studies without clinical outcomes
- Non-COVID conditions without ME/CFS relevance
