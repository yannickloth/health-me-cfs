## Inclusion Criteria
- VMAT2 PET studies (any condition — establishes methodology)
- Dopaminergic imaging (PET, SPECT) in long COVID or ME/CFS
- TSPO PET / neuroinflammation PET in long COVID striatum
- Postmortem evidence of SARS-CoV-2 effects on dopamine neurons
- Dopamine augmentation treatment trials in ME/CFS or long COVID
- Postviral dopaminergic dysfunction (animal models, historical)
- CSF dopamine metabolites in ME/CFS
- Competing mechanisms (mitochondrial, AMPA, noradrenergic)
- Apathy, motor slowing, cognitive slowing as symptoms in ME/CFS
- Null/negative results (TSPO PET normal in CFS, DA metabolites normal)

## Exclusion Criteria
- Purely in vitro studies without human relevance
- Parkinson's disease without ME/CFS or long COVID comparator
- Animal studies of normal dopamine function (not postviral)
- Non-English publications not accessible
- Purely methodological PET development studies
