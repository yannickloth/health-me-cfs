## Inclusion Criteria
- Peer-reviewed primary research, systematic reviews, or narrative reviews
- Directly addresses HS and fatigue, sleep disturbance, or quality of life
- Addresses HS and ME/CFS or fibromyalgia comorbidity
- Addresses NLRP3 inflammasome in HS (indirect mechanistic link to ME/CFS fatigue)
- Addresses NLRP3 inflammasome in ME/CFS or fatigue models
- English language

## Exclusion Criteria
- Case reports of single patients (except Deckers 2016 for mechanistic value)
- Papers where fatigue mentioned only in passing without quantitative data
- Papers addressing urticaria, vitiligo, or other non-HS skin conditions without HS data
- Papers on biologics without fatigue outcomes
- Conference abstracts without full text
