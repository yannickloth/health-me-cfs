## Inclusion Criteria
- Directly relevant to adjudicating the Shor claim: seronegative chronic Lyme as CFS driver, prolonged antibiotic efficacy/inefficacy, diagnostic validity of non-standard Lyme markers
- Peer-reviewed strongly preferred; CDC/MMWR included as authoritative surveillance
- English language
- RCTs, meta-analyses, official position statements, and contradicting evidence prioritized

## Exclusion Criteria
- Already in existing bib (Klempner2001LymeAbx, Fallon2008LymeCeftriaxone, IDSALyme2020, Steere2016postLyme, PTLDSMECFSReview2023, Rogerson2020LymeIDSAILADS)
- Animal studies only
- Duplicates across databases
- Opinion pieces without substantive evidence
