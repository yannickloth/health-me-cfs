## Inclusion Criteria

- Direct EV isolation/cargo studies in ME/CFS (primary target)
- Plasma/blood EV proteomics, miRNA, surface markers in ME/CFS
- Pre-pandemic and post-pandemic EV studies (Rydland 2026 pre-pandemic)
- Circuit: ME/CFS patients meeting Canadian Consensus Criteria, Fukuda, or ICC
- EV-related coagulation/microclot studies in ME/CFS context
- Null/negative findings included

## Exclusion Criteria

- Non-ME/CFS populations without chronic fatigue
- In vitro studies not referencing ME/CFS
- Animal models without human parallel ME/CFS relevance
- Full-text inaccessible, no abstract available
- Reviews with no primary data (exceptions: systematic reviews, comprehensive mechanisms)
- Cancer EV studies not applicable to ME/CFS
