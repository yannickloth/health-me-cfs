## Inclusion Criteria
- Peer-reviewed original research or reviews on antibody persistence mechanisms
- Studies on herpesvirus serology in ME/CFS (any herpesvirus)
- Basic immunology studies on long-lived plasma cells (LLPCs)
- Studies examining null/negative herpesvirus serology in ME/CFS (balance assessment)
- English language
- Any publication date (foundational immunology spans decades)

## Exclusion Criteria
- Non-English without translation
- Meeting abstracts without full text
- Studies exclusively on animal coronaviruses (feline, murine) without human relevance
- Case reports (n<3)
- Papers on acute herpesvirus infection in immunocompromised (transplant, HIV) without relevance to ME/CFS
