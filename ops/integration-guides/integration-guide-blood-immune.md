## Novel vs Confirming Findings

### Novel (not in existing paper):
1. NII-based white matter neuroinflammation evidence (Yu2026)
2. CSF proteomics with POTS/severity stratification (Bragée2026)
3. Galectin-9–TIM-3 as mechanism for gamma-delta/MAIT cell depletion (Shahbaz2026)
4. Complement genetics pQTL approach identifying inflammatory subgroup (Maya2026)
5. EV protein/miRNA signatures as biomarkers (Seifert2026)
6. "Immune remodeling" framing vs simple "immune activation" (multiple papers)
7. Longitudinal proteomics capturing PEM dynamics (Germain2025)
8. Blood-brain barrier / neuroinflammation via diffusion MRI (Yu2026)
9. PC(O-38:4 as near-discriminative lipid biomarker (Lipidaccumulation2025)

### Confirming (reinforces existing content):
1. Energy metabolism dysfunction (ATP/ADP, TCA, beta-oxidation) — Germain2025, Che2025, Heng2025
2. Tryptophan-kynurenine pathway disruption — Che2025
3. Immune cell subset alterations — Shahbaz2026, Heng2025
4. Endothelial dysfunction / vascular abnormalities — Heng2025, Hoel2026
5. Lipid abnormalities — Che2025, Lipidaccumulation2025
6. Post-exertional malaise molecular mechanisms — Germain2025, Che2025
7. Complement involvement — Maya2026, Che2025, Bragée2026
8. TRPM3 dysfunction replication — Sasso2026
