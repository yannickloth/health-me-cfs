## Integration Context

**Priority:** 🟡  
**Tier:** 3  
**ME/CFS links:**
- Gut virome (bacteriophages and enteric viruses) is the most abundant biological entity in the GI tract; absent from current ME/CFS documentation
- Bacteriophage populations modulate bacterial dysbiosis; altered phage diversity drives dysbiotic community selection
- Anellovirus burden (TTV, TTMV) inversely correlates with immune competence; elevation indicates immune dysregulation
- Enteric virus persistence (adenoviruses, astroviruses, SARS-CoV-2, enteroviruses) maintains low-level innate immune activation
- FMT transfers whole virome including donor bacteriophage communities; virome matching may affect FMT outcomes

4. Does FMT with virome-matched donor material improve microbiome engraftment vs standard FMT?
