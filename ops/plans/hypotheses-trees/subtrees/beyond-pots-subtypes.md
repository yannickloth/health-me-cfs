## Integration Context

**Priority:** 🔴 Critical
**Category:** 4 — Cardiovascular and vascular

**ME/CFS links:**
- POTS already covered in paper but needs dedicated subtype integration pass
- Autoimmune POTS: GPCR-Ab (β1-AR, α1-AR) — replication failure needs honest discussion
- Hyperadrenergic POTS: norepinephrine spill-over; NET mutation
- Hypovolemic POTS: blood volume dysregulation; renin-angiotensin
- All three subtypes can co-occur with ME/CFS — mechanism and prevalence data needed

**Target chapters:** ch04-08-differential-diagnosis, ch05-subgroups, ch07-immune-dysfunction, ch14b-autoantibodies, ch14d-cross-disease

**Pre-identified hypotheses:** (GPCR-Ab replication failure must be reported honestly; see ant-autoantibodies subtree)

**Research questions:**
1. What proportion of ME/CFS patients meet formal POTS criteria?
2. Are GPCR-Ab (β1-AR, α1-AR) reliably elevated in autoimmune POTS vs. controls — what do replication studies show?
3. Does autoimmune POTS subtype respond to immunosuppression in ME/CFS comorbid patients?
4. How does hyperadrenergic POTS mechanism differ from the autonomic dysfunction in idiopathic ME/CFS?
5. Does hypovolemia in POTS-ME/CFS drive fatigue independently of autonomic dysfunction (volume expansion trial data)?

**Note:** Distinct from general dysautonomia spectrum (see `subtrees/beyond-dysautonomia.md`). POTS = postural tachycardia specifically; dysautonomia = broader autonomic failure.
