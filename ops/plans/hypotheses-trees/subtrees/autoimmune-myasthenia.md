**Priority:** 🔴 Critical
**Tier:** 1

**ME/CFS links:**
- Neuromuscular junction autoimmunity; AChR and MuSK antibodies
- Fatigable weakness vs. PEM — MG worsens with repetition; ME/CFS worsens with any exertion
- Lambert-Eaton overlap (voltage-gated Ca²⁺ channel antibodies)
- Autonomic features in MG (tachycardia, orthostasis)
- Muscarinic GPCR autoantibody class overlap with ME/CFS

4. Does pyridostigmine (AChE inhibitor) improve ME/CFS-like fatigue?
5. Is Lambert-Eaton a better mechanistic comparator for ME/CFS than MG proper?
