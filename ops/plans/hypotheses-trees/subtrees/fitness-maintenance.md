### 2026-05-26 — Phase 4 integration + lit review

**Integrated:** 0.1–0.6 (parent cycle), 1.1 (COPD/HF cross-disease), 1.2 (bifurcation model) → 8 total.

**Parked (`↩️`):** 10 nodes — all have mechanistic plausibility but zero ME/CFS-specific therapeutic trial data:
- 1.3: Epigenetic exercise-response studies (Bouquet 2019, Che 2025) show abnormal transcription but no direct test of maintenance-mode epigenetic lock.
- 2.1: McCully et al. 2004 (J Appl Physiol, n=19 CFS, 11 controls) — BFR in CFS showed reduced hyperemic flow under occlusion but no metabolic impairment. Owens Recovery Science published clinical reasoning for BFR in Long COVID/PEM (2022, no trial data). Zero BFR therapeutic trials in ME/CFS.
- 2.2: Multiple bed-rest studies (56-day ESA/NASA) confirm resistive vibration exercise reduces lower limb atrophy. No ME/CFS data.
- 2.3: **NULL — AMPD1 refuted.** Maltese et al. 2016 (Genet Mol Res, n=17 CFS, 50 controls) fully sequenced AMPD1, CPT2, PYGM; found no association with CFS onset, susceptibility, or severity.
- 2.4: Novel composite (HR + orthostatic HR + PEM frequency + FUNCAP + genotype). No validation data exists for any part of this composite in combination.
- 2.5–3.4: Rate-limited during lit review (2026-05-26). Insufficient direct evidence to integrate; all remain ↩️ pending dedicated literature search.
