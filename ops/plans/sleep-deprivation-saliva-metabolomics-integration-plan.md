## Notes

- Study limited: n=20 healthy young men; no women, no chronic illness
- Sleep restriction (4 nights -2h) showed NO significant metabolic change vs rested — only total deprivation (24h) produced detectable signal
- Predictive model: 94% accuracy for sleep-deprived vs well-rested
- No ME/CFS data; entirely general population
- Relevant because unrefreshing sleep is a cardinal ME/CFS symptom per all case definitions
- Next phase: large international validation planned (1,000+ samples, shift workers, women, drivers)
