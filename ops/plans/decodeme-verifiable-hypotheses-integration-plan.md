# DecodeME-Verifiable Hypotheses Integration Plan

**Purpose:** Generate hypotheses that can be verified using the DecodeME dataset (n=17,000+ genotyped ME/CFS patients with extensive phenotype questionnaires) — leveraging DecodeME as a verification instrument rather than a source of new findings.

**Target chapters:**
- Part 2 Ch12 (Genetics & Epigenetics) — primary
- Part 4 Ch36 (Proposed Studies) — study designs
- Part 4 Hypothesis Registry — all new entries

- Sex-stratified genetic architecture — not yet formalized
