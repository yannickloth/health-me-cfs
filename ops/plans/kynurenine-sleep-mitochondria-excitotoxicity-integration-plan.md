## Pre-identified Hypotheses

- **Primary hypothesis**: Integrated kynurenine-sleep-mitochondria-excitotoxicity feedback loop (certainty 0.50)
  - Kynurenine pathway activation → NAD+ depletion → mitochondrial dysfunction
  - Sleep disruption → glymphatic clearance impairment → metabolite accumulation
  - ATP depletion → Na+/K+ pump failure → EAAT2 impairment → glutamate excitotoxicity
  - Excitotoxicity → further metabolic stress → loop amplification
