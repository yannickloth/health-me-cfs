# Integration Plan: Myostatin Inhibition via Repurposed Drugs for Muscle Preservation in ME/CFS

**Purpose:** Myostatin (GDF-8) inhibits muscle growth; ACE inhibitors like lisinopril have weak myostatin-inhibiting effects. Bedbound ME/CFS patients on lisinopril for POTS may experience slower muscle CSA decline via incidental myostatin inhibition.
