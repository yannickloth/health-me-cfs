## Notes

- Primary source: Nature study with ~500,000 UK Biobank participants, very high certainty (0.85)
- No ME/CFS-specific sleep-aging data exists — major research gap
- ME/CFS sleep abnormalities: longer time in bed, longer onset latency, decreased efficiency, decreased stage 2 sleep
- Unrefreshing sleep is core ME/CFS symptom — may reflect glymphatic clearance failure
- Cross-disease parallels with Long COVID, fibromyalgia, insomnia
- Immune senescence link: sleep deprivation → telomere attrition → SASP → inflammation
- Epigenetic clocks show U-shaped relationship with sleep duration
- Glymphatic system degrades with age — provides mechanistic link to ME/CFS neuro symptoms
