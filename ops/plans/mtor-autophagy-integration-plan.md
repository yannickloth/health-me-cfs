# mTOR/Autophagy Integration Plan

**Topic:** mTOR/autophagy in ME/CFS, fibromyalgia, and chronic diseases
**Source:** https://www.myboussole.fr/articles/mtor-autophagie-maladies-chroniques
**Slug:** mtor-autophagy
**Date:** 2026-06-09

## Purpose

Integrate mTORC1→ATG13 autophagy block, mTOR→SASP, and AMPK/mTOR balance as a central mechanistic framework connecting autonomic dysfunction (SNS→PKA→AMPK suppression), mitochondrial quality control failure (blocked autophagy/mitophagy), SASP-driven inflammation, and PEM kinetics. Assess rapamycin (sirolimus) as investigational therapy with preliminary signal.

## Target Chapters

- ch06 (energy metabolism, Step 10 dynamics/biogenesis) — primary: mTOR/AMPK balance, autophagy block, nocturnal failure, tone index
- ch07 (immune dysfunction) — mTOR→SASP→IL-1α→NF-κB cascade in endothelial senescence
- ch14 (mechanism-treatment-map) — rapamycin, metformin, berberine entries
- ch14h (TRPM3 channelopathy) — IL-11→mTORC1→PIP2→TRPM3 reinforcement
- ch17 (lifestyle) — TRE, low-carb diet, pre-exercise fasting (covered in existing content)
- ch18 (emerging therapies) — rapamycin detail (covered in existing content)

## Pre-Identified Hypotheses (from source article)

- mTORC1 hyperactivation blocks autophagy via ATG13 Ser258 phosphorylation
- AMPK/mTOR bistable switch is tilted toward mTOR dominance in ME/CFS
- mTOR drives SASP via IL-1α→NF-κB (Laberge 2015)
- Rapamycin pilot (Ruan 2025) shows signal — uncontrolled
- SARS-CoV-2 hijacks PI3K/AKT/mTOR (Fattahi 2022)
- mTORC1 syndrome (TorS) as PAIS unifying paradigm (Bar-Tana 2025)
- Metformin→AMPK→mTOR→autophagy (Fineberg 2025)
