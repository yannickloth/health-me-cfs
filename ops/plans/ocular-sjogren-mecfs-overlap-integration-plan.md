## Purpose

Evaluate/integrate the **ocular-surface dimension** of the Sjögren's syndrome (SjS)–ME/CFS overlap: tear-film proteomics, corneal small-fibre neuropathy (corneal confocal microscopy), keratoconjunctivitis sicca phenotype, and the ocular surface as an accessible diagnostic window for ME/CFS neuro-immune status. Scoped narrowly to *ocular* mechanisms — the general SjS–ME/CFS systemic overlap (M3 muscarinic autoantibodies, sicca-fatigue, seronegative-Sjögren, autonomic) is already integrated in ch14d and ch07.
