## Key Claims

1. Muscle calcium overload (NCX reversal): Wirth/Scheibenbogen — sodium MRI confirmed (PMC9733289)
2. Immune TRPM3 dysfunction: large-scale 2025 confirmation (Frontiers in Medicine)
3. Thalamic T-type calcium channel → alpha intrusion: Timofeev 2015 (PMC4575971)
4. TRPM3 expressed in CNS/brain neurons: Falcón 2021 (PMC7959729)
5. LDN partially restores TRPM3 function: Lohn 2024

## Open Hypothesis (explicitly framed as untested)

TRPM3 dysfunction in thalamic neurons — not yet measured in ME/CFS. Falsifiable by: neuronal cell models, post-mortem brain tissue, or thalamic calcium imaging.

## Epistemic Status

- Muscle calcium cascade: well-supported hypothesis, sodium MRI evidence, drug company startup (Mitodicure) built on it
- TRPM3 immune dysfunction: robustly replicated (2025 large-scale study)
- Thalamic calcium/sleep link: mechanistically supported, not ME/CFS-specific
- Unifying TRPM3 hypothesis: explicitly speculative, framed as open research question
