# Global Plan: New Content Addition Order

**Ordering principle:** Topics are ranked by a composite of three criteria:
1. **Healing potential** — Could this knowledge lead to actionable treatment for patients?
2. **Patient utility** — Does this help patients understand symptoms, seek diagnosis, or manage their condition today?
3. **Research potential** — Does this open new avenues for investigation or fill critical knowledge gaps?

Each topic is scored H/M/L on each criterion. The final order weights healing potential highest, then patient utility, then research potential.

**Excluded from ordering:** `mcp-server.md` (software project, not paper content) and `post-viral-pituitary-injury.md` (already implemented).
