## Meta-Observation

The article's core rhetorical move — disclaiming conspiracy while building a thesis that requires convergent coordination — is its greatest vulnerability. The hedge is a shield, not a method. The structural-observation version and the intentionalist version are mixed throughout. Separating them cleanly would make the piece much harder to attack.
