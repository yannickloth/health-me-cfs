## Inclusion Criteria
1. Peer-reviewed journal articles, systematic reviews, or high-quality preprints (2024+)
2. Direct relevance to ME/CFS, chronic fatigue, or chronic inflammatory illness populations
3. Addresses at least one of: interoceptive processing, emotional recognition/regulation, alexithymia, illness cognitions (catastrophizing), predictive coding of fatigue, sickness behavior as emotional phenotype
4. English-language publications
5. Publication date ≤ 2026

## Exclusion Criteria
1. Animal studies without clear human relevance pathway
2. Conference abstracts only (no full paper)
3. Dissertations/theses
4. Non-English publications without English translation
5. Papers focused solely on cancer-related fatigue without chronic fatigue/ME/CFS relevance
6. Papers on general HRV without emotion or interoception processing focus
