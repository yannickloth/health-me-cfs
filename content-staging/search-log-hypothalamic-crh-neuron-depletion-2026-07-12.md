## Inclusion Criteria
- Post-mortem human hypothalamus CRH neuron quantification (direct or methodological precedent)
- ME/CFS neuropathology (any brain region)
- HPA axis function/dysfunction in ME/CFS (reviews, meta-analyses, primary data)
- Long COVID brain autopsy findings
- Neuroinflammation of hypothalamus in chronic fatigue conditions

## Exclusion Criteria
- Animal studies only (no human relevance)
- Non-CRH hypothalamic neuron types studied in isolation without CRH comparison

- Depression literature where CRH neuron increase is expected (included cross-condition papers for method precedent, excluded pure depression comparisons)
