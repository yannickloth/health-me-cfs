### ANCHOR PAPER

#### 1. Ribeiro et al. 2026 — SARS-CoV-2 spike S1-mediated HIF-2α activation
- **PMID:** 41878417
- **Journal:** Frontiers in Immunology (2026); 17:1770758
- **Design:** Human PCS cohort (n=41) + in vitro HRECs + S1 stimulation
- **Key Results:** Elevated EPO, VEGF, MCP-1 in PCS plasma. Spike S1 → transient HIF-1α + **sustained HIF-2α** → endothelial barrier impairment. Belzutifan rescued barrier integrity.
- **Relevance:** Direct evidence for sustained HIF-2α (not HIF-1α) as driver of endothelial dysfunction in post-viral context
- **Quality:** Medium (0.50). Moderate sample, single cell type (retinal), no replication yet
- **Status:** Already in references.bib; needs research_stream update

### NEW PAPERS

#### 2. Deng et al. 2026 — IRF3 attenuates hypoxia signaling by retaining HIF-α in the cytoplasm
- **PMID:** 41520336
- **Journal:** Cell Reports (2026); 45(1):116815
- **Design:** Mechanistic cell biology study
- **Key Results:** Resting cytoplasmic IRF3 interacts with both HIF-1α and HIF-2α, retaining them in cytoplasm under hypoxia. IRF3 depletion → increased HIF signaling. Antiviral IRF3 and HIF pathways directly intersect.
- **Relevance:** Antiviral signaling (IRF3) directly regulates HIF-α stability, providing mechanistic link between viral infection and sustained HIF-2α
- **Quality:** Medium-High (0.65). Well-controlled mechanistic study in top-cell biology journal. In vitro only, no in vivo validation.
- **Research Stream:** hif2a-endothelial-postviral

#### 3. Couteaudier et al. 2025 — HIF-2 triggers production of highly infectious HCV particles
- **PMID:** 40533570
- **Journal:** Cellular and Molecular Life Sciences (2025); 82(1):241
- **Design:** HCV cell culture model with physiological oxygen
- **Key Results:** HIF-2α knockdown → abolished production of infectious HCV lipoviroparticles. Virus hijacks HIF-2α for morphogenesis under physiological hypoxia.
- **Relevance:** Demonstrates direct viral exploitation of HIF-2α for replication. Mechanism may generalize to other viruses (EBV, HHV-6).
- **Quality:** Medium (0.55). In vitro only, HCV-specific. Novel concept.
- **Research Stream:** hif2a-endothelial-postviral

#### 4. Morris et al. 2025 — Role of HIFs in RSV Infection-Associated Lung Disease
- **PMID:** 40244000
- **Journal:** International Journal of Molecular Sciences (2025); 26(7):3182
- **Design:** RSV infection models, HIF modulation
- **Key Results:** RSV stabilizes HIFs under normoxic conditions. HIF-1α inhibition reduces viral replication. Viral infection actively manipulates HIF pathway.
- **Relevance:** Demonstrates virus-induced HIF stabilization under normoxia (not just hypoxia) — applicable to post-viral HIF-2α persistence
- **Quality:** Medium (0.50). Peer-reviewed. RSV-specific. In vitro and animal model.
- **Research Stream:** hif2a-endothelial-postviral

#### 5. Reiterer et al. 2019 — Acute and chronic hypoxia differentially predispose lungs for metastases
- **PMID:** 31308473
- **Journal:** Scientific Reports (2019); 9(1):10246
- **Design:** Pulmonary EC-specific HIF-1α or HIF-2α deletion mice
- **Key Results:** Acute hypoxia → HIF-1α-dependent EC death + permeability → metastasis. Chronic hypoxia → **HIF-2α-dependent** EC protection + vascular integrity. HIF-2α deletion renders lungs vulnerable.
- **Relevance:** **Fundamental**: HIF-1α = acute/barrier-disruptive, HIF-2α = chronic/barrier-protective. Spike S1 may shift this balance toward pathological HIF-2α signaling.
- **Quality:** Medium (0.60). Elegant genetic models, but murine, metastasis-focused.
- **Research Stream:** hif2a-endothelial-postviral

## Summary by Research Question

### Q1: Spike → sustained HIF-2α mechanism?
**YES.** Ribeiro2026 directly shows spike S1 → transient HIF-1α + sustained HIF-2α in ECs. Reiterer2019 provides conceptual framework: HIF-1α = acute response, HIF-2α = chronic adaptation. **Supporting:** Deng2026 (IRF3-antiviral-HIF link), Morris2025 (virus stabilizes HIF under normoxia).

### Q2: Extension to ME/CFS beyond post-COVID?
**Partial.** No direct studies of EBV/HHV-6 + HIF-2α in endothelial cells found. However, Couteaudier2025 (HCV hijacks HIF-2α) and Morris2025 (RSV stabilizes HIF) suggest viral HIF-2α exploitation is general. **Gap identified:** EBV/HHV-6 + HIF-2α in ECs needs direct study.

### Q3: β2AR autoantibody-negative ~70%?
**YES indirect evidence.** Sandvik2023 and Sørland2021 document ED in ME/CFS without autoantibody specificity. Stein2025 defines β2AR-positive subgroup (~30%). The remaining 70% have ED of unknown mechanism — HIF-2α is a candidate.

### Q4: Belzutifan as rescue therapy?
**YES.** Ribeiro2026 uses belzutifan to rescue barrier integrity in vitro. Sales2026 shows darunavir (different mechanism) also inhibits HIF-2α → barrier protection. **Therapeutic avenue identified.**

### Q5: HIF-2α ↔ endothelial senescence?
**YES.** Shen2024 directly shows NRF2-HIF2α axis regulates EC senescence and junction integrity.

### Q6: HIF-2α ↔ complement/VEGF cross-talk?
**Insufficient direct evidence.** No papers found directly linking complement AND HIF-2α in endothelial context. VEGF-HIF-2α connection is well-established (Ribeiro2026, Bishop2025).

### Q7: HIF-2α in other endothelial beds?
| Bed | Evidence |
|-----|----------|
| BBB | Zhang2026 (ICH/switching), Manukjan2023 (hypoxic OPCs → VEGFA → BBB impairment via HIF, not HIF-2α-specific) |
| Pulmonary | Pasupneti2020 (endothelial HIF-2α prevents emphysema), Reiterer2019 |
| Retinal | Ribeiro2026 (anchor) |
| Cardiac | Reiterer2019 (HIF-2α protects cardiac microvascular barrier) |
