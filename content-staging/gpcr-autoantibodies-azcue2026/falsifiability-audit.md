## spec:muscarinic-cognition
Status: fully falsifiable
Predictions found:
- (a) Functional receptor assays (calcium flux, impedance) should demonstrate whether muscarinic AAbs from ME/CFS sera have agonistic, antagonistic, or null effects on receptor signaling
- (b) If muscarinic AAbs are compensatory partial agonists, their depletion via immunoadsorption should transiently worsen verbal/working memory
- (c) Muscarinic AAb titers should be inversely correlated with CSF acetylcholine metabolites (HVA/MHPG) if the compensatory hypothesis is correct
