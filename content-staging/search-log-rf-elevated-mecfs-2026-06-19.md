## Inclusion Criteria
- Peer-reviewed journal article, preprint, or systematic review
- Addresses rheumatoid factor (RF) in context of ME/CFS, CFS, or chronic fatigue
- Addresses RF in fibromyalgia (as overlapping condition)
- Addresses autoantibody profiling in ME/CFS (as competing context)
- Systematic reviews on autoimmunity in ME/CFS
- English or English abstract available
- Pre-2000 papers included only if no modern replication exists

## Exclusion Criteria
- Papers on RF in rheumatoid arthritis only, without fatigue/CFS relevance
- Papers on fatigue in other autoimmune diseases without RF measurement
- Purely therapeutic RA trials with no ME/CFS applicability
- Case reports without relevance to ME/CFS mechanism
