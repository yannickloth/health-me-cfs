## Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria

### Inclusion
- Any study measuring RF or autoantibodies in ME/CFS/CFS populations
- Studies of RF in fibromyalgia (as comparator condition)
- Autoantibody profiling studies in ME/CFS (context)
- Studies examining fatigue/RF relationship in autoimmune disease (RA, Sjögren)
- Systematic reviews of autoimmunity in ME/CFS
- Studies of post-viral fatigue with autoantibody data

### Exclusion
- RA-focused studies without fatigue outcome data
- Case reports of siliconosis/breast implant illness without ME/CFS context
- Non-English without English abstract
- Studies of purely mechanical/inflammatory arthritis without fatigue measurement
