# Evidence Synthesis: Elevated Rheumatoid Factor + ME/CFS

**Date:** 2026-06-19 | **Topic slug:** rf-elevated-mecfs | **Papers:** 13

## 1. Evidence Landscape

### Strong (certainty ≥ 0.60)
- Germain 2025 (0.65, n=172): Largest ME/CFS autoAb screen — complete null. RF not specifically measured but provides context.

### Weak (certainty < 0.40, and/or small n, single study)
- Hashimoto 1992 (0.25, n=51): Only direct RF measurement in CFS. 11.8% RF+. No controls. Japanese only. Not replicated in 34 years.
- Kerr 2002 (0.40): Post-B19 cohort; RF part of autoAb panel. 5 developed CFS. No RF pattern.
- Rasmussen 1994 (0.30, n=21+21): No autoAb differences in CFS vs controls.

### Missing
- No controlled RF prevalence study in modern ME/CFS cohort
- No RF isotype analysis
- No RF correlation with PEM, severity, or autonomic function
- RF not included in any modern autoAb profiling panel (including Germain 2025)

### Null
- Rasmussen 1994: No autoAb elevation
- Germain 2025: Complete autoAb null (n=172, 7542 antigens)
- Batham 2024: Systematic review — insufficient evidence for autoimmune ME/CFS

## 2. Contradictions
- Indirect tension: Hashimoto 1992 (11.8% RF+) vs Rasmussen 1994 (no autoAb differences). Different eras, populations, criteria. Both small n. Not a fundamental contradiction — unresolvable without modern data.

## 3. Evidence Summary
Only one study in 34 years has directly measured RF in a CFS cohort (Hashimoto 1992, n=51, certainty 0.25). The remaining literature is contextual — autoimmune profiling studies that didn't measure RF, fibromyalgia overlap, and systematic reviews concluding insufficient evidence for autoimmunity. No claim about RF in ME/CFS is evidence-supported.

## 4. Clinical Relevance
- **LOW.** RF testing not indicated in ME/CFS workup except when autoimmune disease suspected.
- **Subset applicability:** Unknown.

## 5. Integration Decision: **DEFER**
Literature is too sparse to support any claim about RF in ME/CFS. The direct evidence consists of one unreplicated 1992 case series. Contextual papers are null or address different conditions. No chapter modifications warranted.
