## Inclusion Criteria
- Studies examining food insecurity, food access, or nutritional security in ME/CFS or Long COVID
- Studies documenting socioeconomic burden of ME/CFS (employment loss, disability, poverty)
- Systematic reviews of dietary/nutritional interventions in ME/CFS
- Peer-reviewed publications (preprints included if recent and relevant)
- English language

## Exclusion Criteria
- Animal studies without relevance to human food security
- Dietary supplementation studies without food security context
- General population food insecurity studies without chronic illness focus
- Opinion pieces, letters, editorials
