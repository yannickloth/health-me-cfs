## Case Summary

Female patient, age 50, with severe ME/CFS presenting a distinctive "exhausted immune surveillance" phenotype characterized by:
- Severe B-cell depletion (CD19+ 0.05 G/l)
- Extremely elevated EBV titers (596 E/ml)
- Low NK cells (6.89%)
- Elevated T-cells (85%)
- Multiple comorbidities including MCAS (tryptase-negative), POTS, IC, SFN, Fibromyalgia

## Unique Features

1. **Inverted immune pattern**: Low B-cells with high T-cells (opposite of typical)
2. **Multi-hit disease trajectory**: 2012 IC → 2014 infection → 2015 fibro → 2021 vaccine reactions → 2022 ME/CFS → 2024-25 viral cascade → bedbound
3. **Paradoxical medication reactor**: LDN, Famotidine, Prednisolone all caused psychiatric adverse effects
4. **Cimetidine responder**: Dramatic improvement with Luc Biland protocol ("got me out of bed")
5. **Mestinon sensitivity**: 20mg tolerated, 60mg caused severe symptoms
