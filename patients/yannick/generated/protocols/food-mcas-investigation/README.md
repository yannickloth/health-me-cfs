# Food-Triggered MCAS Investigation Protocol

**Started:** 2026-03-17
**Status:** Phase 1 — Food Diary (baseline observation)

## Hypothesis

Silent (non-IgE, no visible allergic symptoms) food-triggered mast cell
degranulation may be contributing to ME/CFS symptoms — fatigue, brain fog,
joint pain, autonomic dysfunction — without producing recognizable allergic
reactions.

## Phases

| Phase | Period | Goal | Status |
|-------|--------|------|--------|
| 1. Food Diary | Weeks 1-4 (Mar 17 - Apr 13) | Baseline data collection | **ACTIVE** |
| 2. Pattern Analysis | Week 5 (Apr 14-20) | Identify suspect foods | Pending |
| 3. Targeted Elimination | Weeks 6-9+ (Apr 21 - May 30) | Confirm/exclude triggers | Pending |
| 4. Medical Confirmation | Weeks 8-10 (if needed) | Urine mediator testing | Pending |
