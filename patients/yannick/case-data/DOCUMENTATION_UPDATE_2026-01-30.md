### 6. Literature References Added
**File**: `references.bib`

Added three new references:
1. **Luke_2001**: Fluoride deposition in aged human pineal gland (Caries Research)
   - Human autopsy study, n=11
   - Mean pineal fluoride 9000 ppm (range 2900-21,800 ppm)
   - Higher than bone; age-related accumulation
   - Correlation with pineal calcification

2. **US_NRC_2006**: Fluoride in Drinking Water (National Research Council)
   - Authoritative review on fluoride health effects
   - "Fluoride is likely to cause decreased melatonin production"
   - Mechanism: enzyme inhibition in melatonin synthesis
   - Children particularly vulnerable (80-90% retention)

3. **Bateman_2024_Concussion_ME_CFS**: Concussion history associated with increased ME/CFS risk
   - ME/CFS patients have 4.89× higher concussion odds
   - Establishes TBI as significant ME/CFS risk factor
   - Mechanism: persistent autonomic dysfunction post-TBI
   - DAI can damage brainstem autonomic nuclei

## Clinical Implications

### Treatment Targets Suggested by Multi-Hit Model:

1. **Sleep/Circadian Optimization**
   - Melatonin supplementation (address hypothesized fluoride effect)
   - Sleep architecture improvement
   - Chronotherapy approaches

2. **Autonomic Support**
   - Orthostatic intolerance interventions (hydration, salt, compression, medications)
   - Vagal tone modulation (carefully, given hypersensitivity)
   - Heart rate variability biofeedback

3. **HPA Axis Support**
   - Stress management and pacing
   - Potential cortisol support if indicated

4. **Neuroprotection/Neuroplasticity**
   - Post-TBI recovery interventions
   - Neuroplasticity enhancement strategies

5. **Multi-Modal Approach**
   - Multiple system involvement suggests single interventions insufficient
   - Addressing cumulative burden may provide gradual improvement
