## Key Findings Summary

1. **Psychologization prevalence:** 46.8% of LC patients told symptoms are psychological (Büchner 2025)
2. **Stigma burden:** 37% above threshold for clinically relevant stigma in LC (Pantelic 2022); 62% in ME/CFS (Froehlich 2022)
3. **Iatrogenic harm:** GET associated with 22-45% dropout, 15-54% worsening rates vs 5-12% for pacing (Cooper 2025)
4. **Epistemic injustice:** Both testimonial (symptoms dismissed) and hermeneutical (no conceptual resources for novel illness) documented in LC and ME/CFS (Byrne 2020, Ireson 2022)
5. **Framework:** Stigmatisation follows labeling→discrediting→othering→discrimination cycle in PPS/FD (Treufeldt 2024)
