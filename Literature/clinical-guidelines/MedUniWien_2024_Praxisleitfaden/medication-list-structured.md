## Acronym Verification

**CRITICAL ACRONYMS:**
- **LDA:** Low-Dose Aripiprazole (NOT Low-Dose Aspirin)
- **LDN:** Low-Dose Naltrexone
- **TASS:** Thrombo-ASS (Aspirin)
- **PAIS:** Post-Acute Infection Syndromes
- **PEM:** Post-Exertional Malaise
- **PoTS:** Postural Tachycardia Syndrome
- **MCAS:** Mast Cell Activation Syndrome

---

## Dosing Principles (from Praxisleitfaden)

1. Start with lowest dose
2. Increase slowly if symptom improvement and no adverse effects
3. Discontinue if adverse effects occur
4. Trial dose reduction/discontinuation over time
5. Account for therapy when ordering diagnostic tests (to avoid confounding)
6. ME/CFS patients often report medication intolerances
7. No standard dosing schema fits all patients
