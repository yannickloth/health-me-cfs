# The Energy Envelope Theory and ME/CFS (Jason et al. 2008)

**Citation:** Jason, L.A., Muldowney, K., & Torres-Harding, S. (2008). The Energy Envelope Theory and myalgic encephalomyelitis/chronic fatigue syndrome. *AAOHN Journal, 56*(5), 189-195.

**DOI:** 10.3928/08910162-20080501-06
**PMID:** 18578185
**BibTeX key:** `Jason2008EnergyEnvelope`

## Overview

Foundational paper establishing the Energy Envelope Theory and Energy Quotient metric for ME/CFS activity management.

## Key Contribution

Developed and validated the Energy Quotient formula:
```
Energy Quotient = (Expended Energy / Perceived Energy) Ã— 100
```

Demonstrated that patients exceeding their energy envelope (quotient >100%) experienced significantly worse outcomes across multiple health indices.

## Evidence Grade: A

- Large sample (n=114)
- 15-year validation period
- Multiple outcome measures
- Peer-reviewed publication
