# Energy Modulation Impact on Functioning (Jason et al. 2009)

**Citation:** Jason, L.A., Benton, M., Torres-Harding, S., & Muldowney, K. (2009). The impact of energy modulation on physical functioning and fatigue severity among patients with ME/CFS. *Patient Education and Counseling, 77*(2), 237-241.

**DOI:** 10.1016/j.pec.2009.02.015
**PMID:** 19356884
**PMCID:** PMC2767446
**BibTeX key:** `Jason2009EnergyModulation`

## Overview

Longitudinal intervention study demonstrating that ME/CFS patients who successfully stay within their energy envelope show significant improvements in functioning over time.
