Health Rising (2025). ME/CFS Autopsy Study Finds a Wrecked HPA Axis: The 2025 IACFS Conference. Health Rising blog. URL: https://www.healthrising.org/blog/2025/12/04/chronic-fatigue-hpa-axis-autopsy/

Abstract:
University of Amsterdam autopsy study of 7-10 ME/CFS patients found very low levels of CRH-producing neurons in hypothalamus, indicating dramatic CRH depletion and HPA axis dysfunction. Presented at IACFS 2025 conference. No mention of striatum, eMSN, or basal ganglia findings. Provides preliminary evidence for hypothalamic CRH depletion in ME/CFS.
