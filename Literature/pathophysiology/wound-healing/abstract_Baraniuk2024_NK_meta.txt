PMID: 39483457
Title: Meta-analysis of natural killer cell cytotoxicity in myalgic encephalomyelitis/chronic fatigue syndrome
Authors: Baraniuk JN, Eaton-Fitch N, Marshall-Gradisnik S
Journal: Frontiers in Immunology
Year: 2024
Volume: 15
Pages: 1440643
DOI: 10.3389/fimmu.2024.1440643

Abstract:
This meta-analysis examined 28 papers containing 55 data points on NK cell function in ME/CFS. The researchers
confirmed that NK cell dysfunction is "the most consistent immune finding in ME/CFS." NK cell cytotoxicity
was significantly reduced to about half of healthy control levels, with an overall Hedges' g of 0.96
(95% CI: 0.75-1.18). The analysis confirmed reproducible NK cell dysfunction across multiple studies,
despite significant heterogeneity attributable to varying methodology and effector-to-target cell ratios.
