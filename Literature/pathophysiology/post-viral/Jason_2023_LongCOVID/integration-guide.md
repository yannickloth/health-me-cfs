## Key Points to Convey

1. **13-45% overlap** - significant proportion of Long COVID is ME/CFS
2. **Multi-system** - shared abnormalities across 7+ biological systems
3. **Authority** - Komaroff is preeminent ME/CFS researcher; Lipkin is renowned virologist
4. **Validation** - Long COVID research validates ME/CFS findings
5. **Treatment implications** - successful Long COVID interventions may help ME/CFS and vice versa

## Certainty Assessment for Integration

- **Quality:** High (comprehensive review, authoritative authors, peer-reviewed)
- **Sample:** Review synthesizing multiple studies
- **Replication:** Consistent findings across independent studies
- **Limitations:** Long COVID heterogeneous; overlap percentage varies by diagnostic criteria used
