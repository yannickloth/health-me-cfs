# Cardiovascular Autonomic Dysregulation and Retina in MS (de Rodez Benavent et al. 2019)

**Citation:** de Rodez Benavent SA, Nygaard GO, Nilsen KB, et al. Neurodegenerative interplay of cardiovascular autonomic dysregulation and the retina in early multiple sclerosis. *Frontiers in Neurology*. 2019;10:507.

**DOI:** 10.3389/fneur.2019.00507
**PMID:** 31156539
**BibTeX key:** `derodezbenavent2019autonomic`

## Key Contribution
Integrates cardiovascular autonomic testing with ocular assessment in early MS. Demonstrates link between systemic autonomic dysregulation and ocular manifestations. Supports multimodal autonomic assessment including pupillary measures.

## Evidence Grade: C+
- Moderately sized, well-characterized cohort
- Cross-sectional
- Integrates autonomic + ocular measures
- Supports rationale for ocular autonomic biomarkers
