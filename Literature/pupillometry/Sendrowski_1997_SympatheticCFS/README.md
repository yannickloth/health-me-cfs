# Sympathetic Hypersensitivity in CFS (Sendrowski et al. 1997)

**Citation:** Sendrowski DP, Buker EA, Gee SS. An investigation of sympathetic hypersensitivity in chronic fatigue syndrome. *Optometry and Vision Science*. 1997;74(8):660-663.

**DOI:** 10.1097/00006324-199708000-00027
**PMID:** 9323737
**BibTeX key:** `sendrowski1997sympathetic`

## Key Contribution
Only direct pupillometry study in CFS. Tests sympathetic denervation hypersensitivity hypothesis using pupillary measures. CFS patients show similar SPECT patterns to fibromyalgia (sympathetic hypersensitivity condition).

## Evidence Grade: D
- Very small sample
- Outdated methodology (1997)
- Single study, not replicated
- Historically important but low certainty for modern use
- Remains the only direct pupillary autonomic assessment in CFS
