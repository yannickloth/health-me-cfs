# CD26 and GPCR Autoantibodies in ME/CFS (Szklarski et al. 2021)

**Citation:** Szklarski M, Freitag H, Lorenz S, et al. Delineating the association between soluble CD26 and autoantibodies against G-protein coupled receptors... *Frontiers in Immunology*. 2021;12:644548.

**DOI:** 10.3389/fimmu.2021.644548
**PMID:** 33889154
**BibTeX key:** `szklarski2021cd26`

## Key Contribution
CRITICAL INDIRECT LINK: ME/CFS patients with infection-triggered onset show elevated autoantibodies against alpha-1 adrenergic and M3 muscarinic receptors - the exact receptors mediating pupillary dilation (alpha-1) and constriction (M3). Establishes biochemical bridge between pupil control and ME/CFS autoimmunity.

## Evidence Grade: B+
- Large ME/CFS cohort
- Peer-reviewed (Front Immunol)
- Independent value beyond pupillometry
- Key paper linking pupil autonomic receptors to ME/CFS
