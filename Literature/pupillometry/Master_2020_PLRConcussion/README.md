# PLR as Biomarker for Adolescent Concussion (Master et al. 2020)

**Citation:** Master CL, Podolak OE, Ciuffreda KJ, et al. Utility of pupillary light reflex metrics as a physiologic biomarker for adolescent sport-related concussion. *JAMA Ophthalmology*. 2020;138(11):1135-1141.

**DOI:** 10.1001/jamaophthalmol.2020.3466
**PMID:** 32970102
**BibTeX key:** `master2020plr`

## Key Contribution
Large-scale validation of PLR metrics as objective biomarker (n=352). Demonstrates PLR distinguishes concussed from controls. Provides strong precedent for PLR as neurologic/autonomic biomarker.

## Evidence Grade: A-
- Large sample (n=352)
- High-impact journal (JAMA Ophthalmol)
- Strong study design with controls
- Directly validates PLR as diagnostic biomarker
