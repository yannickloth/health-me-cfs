# Scotopic-Photopic Pupil Responses in Diabetes (Li et al. 2026)

**Citation:** Li Y, Pan Z, Ren Q, Shi F, Wan P. The difference in scotopic and photopic pupil responses: a potential indicator for long-term glycemic management. *Frontiers in Neurology*. 2026;17:1785905.

**DOI:** 10.3389/fneur.2026.1785905
**PMID:** 42388712
**BibTeX key:** `li2026pupil`

## Key Contribution
Validates scotopic-photopic pupil diameter difference as biomarker for diabetic autonomic neuropathy. ROC analysis confirms diagnostic performance for chronicity assessment. Demonstrates pupillometry as quantitative autonomic neuropathy biomarker.

## Evidence Grade: B-
- Good sample size
- ROC-based diagnostic validation
- Cross-sectional, single study
- Directly demonstrates pupil-based autonomic neuropathy assessment
