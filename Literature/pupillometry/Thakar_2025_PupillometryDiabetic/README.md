# Pupillometry in Diabetic Patients (Thakar et al. 2025)

**Citation:** Thakar M, Tripathy SP, Dutta P, Bhattacharya S, Dhaka U. Quantitative automated pupillometry in diabetic patients and correlation with retinal nerve fibre layer thickness. *Eye*. 2025;39(10):1983-1989.

**DOI:** 10.1038/s41433-025-03793-3
**PMID:** 40211015

## Key Contribution
Quantitative pupillometry correlates PLR parameters with retinal nerve fiber layer thickness in diabetic patients. Demonstrates structural correlate of pupillary autonomic dysfunction.
