# Plasmapheresis/IVIG in ME/CFS with M3 Antibodies (Oesch-Régeni et al. 2025)

**Citation:** Oesch-Régeni B, Germann N, Hafer G, Schmid D, Arn N. The effect on quality of life of therapeutic plasmapheresis and intravenous immunoglobulins... *Journal of Clinical Medicine*. 2025;14(11):3802.

**DOI:** 10.3390/jcm14113802
**PMID:** 40507564
**BibTeX key:** `oeschregeni2025m3`

## Key Contribution
Demonstrates M3-muscarinic receptor autoantibodies are clinically actionable in ME/CFS. Patients with elevated M3 antibodies treated with plasmapheresis/IVIG. Establishes clinical relevance of iris sphincter (M3) pathway in ME/CFS.

## Evidence Grade: D+
- Pilot study, no control group
- Small sample (n=10)
- No pupillometry measurements
- Important for establishing M3 autoantibody clinical relevance
