# Diagnostic Significance of Pupillary Reflex Pathways (Lisowski et al. 2025)

**Citation:** Lisowski Ł, Lisowska J, Charytonowicz A, et al. The diagnostic significance of pupillary reflex pathways: insights from classical examination and advanced pupillometry. *Frontiers in Neuroscience*. 2025;19:1677431.

**DOI:** 10.3389/fnins.2025.1677431
**PMID:** 41169745
**BibTeX key:** `lisowski2025pupillary`

## Key Contribution
Comprehensive review of pupillary reflex anatomy and physiology. Covers Edinger-Westphal nucleus, ciliary ganglion, M3 muscarinic sphincter, alpha-1 adrenergic dilator. Essential anatomical framework for pupillometry as autonomic assessment.

## Evidence Grade: B
- Review article, no original data
- Comprehensive anatomical coverage
- Provides essential framework for understanding pupil autonomic control
