# Pupillary Hippus as Biomarker (Rizzuto et al. 2025)

**Citation:** Rizzuto V, Laurino M, Montanari R, et al. Pupillary hippus as a biomarker: spectral signatures and complexity approaches in autonomic and clinical contexts. *Bioengineering*. 2025;12(12):1376.

**DOI:** 10.3390/bioengineering12121376

## Key Contribution
Comprehensive narrative review synthesizing spectral and nonlinear pupillometry studies. Low-frequency pupillary oscillations covary with autonomic rhythms. Proposes unified taxonomy of hippus frequency bands.
