# GPCR Autoantibodies in Post-COVID and CFS (Azcue et al. 2026)

**Citation:** Azcue N, Prada A, Del Pino R, et al. Involvement of autoantibodies against G protein-coupled receptors in post-COVID condition and chronic fatigue syndrome. *Scientific Reports*. 2026;16(1):11837.

**DOI:** 10.1038/s41598-026-49131-9
**PMID:** 42082542
**BibTeX key:** `azcue2026gpcr`

## Key Contribution
Large-cohort evidence for GPCR autoantibodies including anti-alpha-1 adrenergic in post-COVID and CFS. Provides evidence for autoimmune-mediated autonomic dysfunction targeting the same receptors that control pupillary function.

## Evidence Grade: B+
- Large cohort
- Published in reputable journal (Sci Rep)
- Directly relevant to both post-COVID and ME/CFS
- Replicates and extends prior GPCR autoantibody findings
