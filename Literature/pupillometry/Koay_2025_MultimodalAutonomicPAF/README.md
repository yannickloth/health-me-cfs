# Multimodal Autonomic Biomarkers Predict Phenoconversion in PAF (Koay et al. 2025)

**Citation:** Koay S, Vichayanrat E, Bremner F, et al. Multimodal autonomic biomarkers predict phenoconversion in pure autonomic failure. *Annals of Clinical and Translational Neurology*. 2025;12(11):2170-2180.

**DOI:** 10.1002/acn3.70140
**PMID:** 40696849
**BibTeX key:** `koay2025multimodal`

## Key Contribution
Demonstrates pupillometry as component of multimodal autonomic biomarker panel. Supports role of pupillary assessment in longitudinal autonomic monitoring.

## Evidence Grade: B
- Longitudinal design with phenoconversion endpoint
- Published in reputable neurology journal
- Moderate sample size
