# Validation of Monocular Pupillometry in Autonomic Failure (Sander et al. 2025)

**Citation:** Sander L, Oommen G, Brophy C, et al. Validation of monocular pupillometry in healthy controls and patients with autonomic dysfunction: pupillary biomarkers for autonomic failure. *European Journal of Neurology*. 2025;32(8):e70320.

**DOI:** 10.1111/ene.70320
**PMID:** 40831073
**BibTeX key:** `sander2025pupillometry`

## Key Contribution
Prospective validation of handheld monocular pupillometer as autonomic screening tool. Establishes normative PLR values and confirms pupillary biomarkers distinguish autonomic failure patients from controls.

## Evidence Grade: B+
- Prospective design, but moderate sample size
- Published in top neurology journal (Eur J Neurol)
- Directly validates pupillometry as autonomic diagnostic tool
- Needs independent replication
