Hu J, Hesson A, Haefner HK, Rominski S. The prevalence of self-reported medical comorbidities in patients with vulvar lichen sclerosus: A single-center retrospective study. International Journal of Gynecology and Obstetrics. 2021;153(2):340-343. doi: 10.1002/ijgo.13480. PMID: 33184843.

ABSTRACT:
Retrospective analysis of intake questionnaires from University of Michigan Center for Vulvar Diseases (1996-2019). 865 women with vulvar LS vs. 1,118 without VLS. Fibromyalgia significantly associated with VLS in univariate analysis but did NOT remain significant in multiple regression model — only age, thyroid disease, and anorectal fissures showed strong association (P<0.01). Self-reported data; single center. Interstitial cystitis also elevated in VLS patients. Chronic fatigue syndrome not separately examined.
