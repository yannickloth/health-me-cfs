Halonen P, Heikinheimo O, Hadkhale K, Gissler M, Pukkala E, Jakobsson M. Risk Factors for Lichen Sclerosus: A Case-Control Study of 43,000 Finnish Women. Journal of Lower Genital Tract Disease. 2024;28(2):164-168. doi: 10.1097/LGT.0000000000000796. PMID: 38518214. PMC: PMC11520338.

ABSTRACT:
Case-control study using nationwide Finnish healthcare registries (1998-2016). 10,692 women with LS vs. 32,076 age-matched controls (3:1 ratio). Mean age at diagnosis 60.8 years. Strong risk associations: morphea (OR 15.1), lichen planus (OR 10.3), alopecia (OR 6.86), vitiligo (OR 2.20), Crohn's disease (OR 1.80), celiac disease (OR 1.49), urge incontinence (OR 1.79), stress incontinence (OR 1.28). FIBROMYALGIA: OR 0.85 (95% CI 0.61-1.18) — NOT significantly associated. Type 1 diabetes (OR 0.43), RA (OR 0.38), CAD (OR 0.41): all PROTECTIVE/negatively associated. Interstitial cystitis elevated when diagnosed <5 years before LS (OR 2.38). Null results for fibromyalgia and socioeconomic status.
