# Gulin et al. 2023 — LS Comorbidity: Swedish Retrospective Cohort

**Authors:** Gulin SJ, Lundin F, Seifert O
**Journal:** European Journal of Medical Research, 2023, Vol. 28, p. 338
**DOI:** 10.1186/s40001-023-01335-9
**PMID:** 37697418
**PMC:** PMC10494448

## Key Points
- Large Swedish registry study: 5,680 LS vs. 362,568 controls
- Strong cancer associations (vulvar, penile, prostate, breast)
- Confirms autoimmune comorbidities: vitiligo, alopecia areata, Crohn's, T1DM
- ME/CFS and fibromyalgia NOT examined — another null result by omission
- No chronic pain/fatigue data reported

## Methodology
Retrospective cohort; Swedish National Patient Registry; ICD-10 L90.0 for LS identification; 2001-2021.

## Certainty Assessment
- **Study Quality:** High
- **Sample Size:** n=5,680 LS cases
- **Replication:** Consistent with US data (Kassels 2024) for autoimmune comorbidities
- **Conflicts of Interest:** Not reported
