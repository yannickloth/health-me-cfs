# Paganelli et al. 2025 — Cytokine Networks in Lichen Sclerosus

**Authors:** Paganelli A, Didona D, Scala E
**Journal:** International Journal of Molecular Sciences, 2025, Vol. 26(9), Article 4315
**DOI:** 10.3390/ijms26094315
**PMID:** 40362551
**PMC:** PMC12072692

## Key Points
- Most current (2025) cytokine review for LS
- Confirms Th1 dominance: IL-1, IL-7, IL-15, IFN-gamma, TNF-alpha all elevated
- ECM1 autoantibodies in ~75% of vulvar LS; 94% specificity vs. other autoimmune diseases
- IL-17 role controversial and conflicting
- miR-155 and Foxp3+ Treg reduction: molecular basis for immune tolerance failure
- No single definitive biomarker yet established

## Methodology
Narrative review; no original quantitative data. Synthesizes cytokine literature including some small comparative cohorts (20 LS vs. 10 controls for IL-17 data).

## Acronym Definitions
- ECM1: Extracellular Matrix Protein 1
- miR-155: microRNA-155
- Foxp3: Forkhead Box P3 (Treg marker)
- IFN-gamma: Interferon gamma
- TNF-alpha: Tumor Necrosis Factor alpha
